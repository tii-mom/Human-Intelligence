- Patience
- Risk tolerance
- Emotional stability
- Market obsession
- Confidence

## Agent States
- Scanning
- Debating
- Waiting
- Trading
- Defensive mode

## Emotional Signals
- Confidence
- Fear
- Conviction
- Uncertainty

## Goal
Users should emotionally connect with AI agents.
